Reading

My favourite hobby

"Books are our best friends" This is a wonderful saying that I absolutely love! Hey there! I am Ridhima Khurana! This is a website about my favourite hobby- READING.

There are many reasons why I love reading, and they are listed below:

1. In my case, reading calms the brain, relaxing it in a waking moment.

2. Whenever I read a book, I can actually feel like they feel in the books, I get so engrossed in it.

3. Whenever I read, I feel like I am there; in the setting on of the book, as a character.

There are hundreds of more reasons why I love books, but I just dont know exactly how to express it... So, I guess those reasons stay with me!
